Erfan Nourbakhsh
Ph.D. Student in Computer Science · University of Texas at San Antonio (UTSA)
📧 erfan.nourbakhsh@utsa.edu

Erfan Nourbakhsh is a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Texas at San Antonio (UTSA). He also serves as a University Instructor, teaching Object-Oriented Programming, and works as a Graduate Research Assistant.

His research focuses on Natural Language Processing (NLP), Graph Neural Networks (GNN), Medical NLP, Biomedical Informatics, AI for Education, and Multimodal AI. He takes an interdisciplinary approach spanning these areas to develop intelligent systems that address challenges in healthcare, education, and multimodal understanding. His work has been accepted at venues including BioNLP, GEM, and IEEE SaTC.

He was selected for the DAIR3 Program, received the Fan Favorite Award at the Draper Data Science Business Plan Competition and the Outstanding and Innovative Research Award at GSAW 2026, and serves as a reviewer for GEM 2026. Beyond academia, he brings industry experience as an AI Specialist, Machine Learning Engineer, and Full-Stack Developer.
